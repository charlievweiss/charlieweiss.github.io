Stories to Orion

These are stories I started writing to my little brother, Orion. He's twelve years my junior and three thousand miles away while I'm in college, so it's a nice way to keep in touch.

They follow the story of a little girl named Halcyon, who grows up to be the Pirate Queen in a novel I plan on writing. As for now, she lives in my head, in a world called Rzk, but you don't know that yet. These letters are a fun way to hash out her backstory in real time as Orion grows up (at this point, Halcy is also seven years old), and teach that kiddo some nice wholesome morals through the power of imagination.
