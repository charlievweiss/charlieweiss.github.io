about me

I am Charlie Weiss.

Engineer, designer, fabricator, artist, etc. I like making good things.
<-- That's me!

I'm currently studying engineering and design at Olin College of Engineering and doing work with New American Public Art, a Boston-based engineer+artist crew who make amazing kinetic sculptures all around the country. You can check out their work on their website. It's awesome stuff.

A World of Play

I love making things that are interesting, durable, well-documented, and useful, but I what I really go for in my work is play. Whether from the creators or the intended audience, play creates an atmosphere of exploration, creativity, and fun, which are what we need to make this world a good place to live.
